Manuscript Preparation/Formatting Guidelines
1. Every manuscript will be subjected to the "similarity check". Manuscripts that fail this check would be rejected without a formal review.
2. DISCOVER would follow double blind review process and hence, author details such as name, designation, affiliation, etc must NOT be included in the manuscript.
3. Since the proceedings of DISCOVER may be published by IEEE CPS (confirmation awaited), the manuscript must adhere to the standard IEEE two column format.
4. Typesetting instructions and sample templates: IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings
5. Manuscript must be submitted in PDF (Portable Document Format) only.
6. Only the manuscript submitted via Easychair link will be considered for review.
7. Formatting instructions for Camera Ready Paper (CRC) will be made available later.
Manuscript Submission Procedure
1. If you do not have an easychair account, please create one here: Sign up for an Easychair Account
2. Submit your paper through this link: DISCOVER 2016 Paper Submission
3. Add details of every author in easychair submission forms.
4. On successful submission, you will receive an email confirmation about the receipt of manuscript.
5. You can update and re-upload your manuscript till the "Paper Submission Deadline".
6. Do not submit your updated manuscript as a "new paper".
